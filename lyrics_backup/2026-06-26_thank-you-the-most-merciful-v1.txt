[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Mercy flowing in the silence of the night
I turn my soul toward Your guiding light
For every breath, for every path I see
Your grace is all the heart will ever need

[Verse 1]
The stars are silent witnesses to Your decree
The universe bows in deep harmony
I wake beneath the velvet blanket of the dark
Seeking the warmth of Your eternal spark
The mundane world dissolves into the dust
And leaves me standing in a state of trust
The beauty of Your creation starts to bloom
Dispelling all the traces of my gloom

[Pre-Chorus]
The world is sleeping, but my spirit wakes
A humble servant for the mercy that You make
The rhythm echoes in the pulse of time
Turning my longing into the sublime

[Chorus]
Thank You for the mercy that restores my soul
الرَّحْمَٰنُ You make the broken spirit whole
I lift my gratitude with every breath I take
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ for all the love for Your sake

[Verse 2]
When I was lost within the maze of pride
You opened up the doors and turned the tide
Each flaw I hide, You cover with Your care
A gentle answer to my silent prayer
Submission is the comfort that I find
Leaving the heavy, weary chains behind
I walk the earth, yet gaze upon the sky
Where truth is written and the shadows die

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The rhythm pulses like a beating heart
Setting my spirit and the world apart
The stillness calls me, and I answer now
With hope upon my lips and truth within my vow

[Chorus]
Thank You for the mercy that restores my soul
الرَّحْمَٰنُ You make the broken spirit whole
I lift my gratitude with every breath I take
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ for all the love for Your sake

[Verse 3]
Testimony rising from the depths of who I am
A traveler finding refuge in the Lamb of Your peace
The journey changes, but the Anchor stays the same
I find my power in the mention of Your Name
The joy is overflowing, liquid gold
A story of Your goodness to be told
The narrow path is wide enough for me
To walk in faith and set the spirit free

[Pre-Chorus 3]
The silence speaks, the universe resounds
Within the love where infinite mercy abounds
A final rise, a crescendo in the chest
Returning to the place where I find rest

[Chorus]
Thank You for the mercy that restores my soul
الرَّحْمَٰنُ You make the broken spirit whole
I lift my gratitude with every breath I take
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ for all the love for Your sake

[Bridge]
Saeed bows his head as the universe exhales, surrendering his will where the ego fails.
The whispers of the night become a prayer, for You alone have caught me in Your care.

[Final Chorus]
Thank You for the mercy that restores my soul
الرَّحْمَٰنُ You make the broken spirit whole
I lift my gratitude with every breath I take
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ for all the love for Your sake
الرَّحْمَٰنُ ... الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ ...
Peace remains.
